Meet Our Team

The talented individuals behind the LED Resin Cube project

Our team combines expertise in electronics, programming, and design to create the interactive LED Resin Cube. Each member brought unique skills that made this project possible.

Parker Ackerknecht

Team Lead

Parker effectively managed the team and ensured all development schedules were on track to meet the numerous deadlines that we faced. Their expertise in electrical engineering allowed us to develop a functional system.

Soldering and PCB design were critical to our success which Parker took into their own hands to create the circuits needed for the project.

PCB Design Circuit Design Soldering

Prakash Gurung

Hardware Lead

Prakash designed and implemented the power circuit for the resin and the wand. He was able to combine wireless charging with a robust power delivery system to ensure all electronics received adequate power in the block.

His systems are able to function without user input and have built in safety features to prevent overheating of the cube.

CAD Materials Science Power Circuit Design

Alec Steiner DeGraaf

Software Lead

Alec lead the software development for the cube. He created the LED animations and the tracking and gesture recogniton algorithm for the cube.

His expertise in Python and Linux allowed him to successfully make the Raspberry Pi function as the controller of the resin cube and ensure the signal integrity of the IR system.

Embedded Systems Web Design Linux Administration
