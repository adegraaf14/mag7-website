Meet Our Team

The talented individuals behind the LED Resin Cube project

Our team combines expertise in electronics, programming, and design to create the interactive LED Resin Cube. Each member brought unique skills that made this project possible.

Jane Doe

Electronics Engineer

Jane led the circuit design and implementation for the LED Resin Cube. With a background in embedded systems and PCB design, she created the custom control board that powers the cube's interactive features.

Her expertise in power management and signal integrity ensured that the complex 3D LED matrix functioned reliably within the resin medium. Jane also designed the IR sensor system that allows the cube to respond to the wand controller.

Circuit Design PCB Layout Embedded Systems

John Smith

Software Developer

John handled all the programming aspects of the LED Resin Cube. He developed the firmware for the microcontroller that creates the various lighting patterns and animations displayed by the cube.

His implementation of the IR signal processing algorithms allows the cube to respond intelligently to different gestures from the wand controller. John also created an efficient memory management system to enable complex animations despite the limited resources of the embedded system.

C/C++ Programming Embedded Firmware Signal Processing

Alex Johnson

Materials Specialist

Alex was responsible for the resin casting process that encapsulates the LED matrix. Their expertise in materials science was crucial for selecting the appropriate resin type that would provide optical clarity while protecting the electronic components.

They developed a custom layer-by-layer casting technique that allowed the LEDs to be suspended in precise positions within the cube. Alex also created the molds and refined the curing process to ensure a perfect final product without bubbles or defects.

Resin Casting Materials Science Prototype Design
